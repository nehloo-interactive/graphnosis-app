## What Graphnosis does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Your AI's hippocampus" icon="star">
    AI has always had the reasoning layer — the prefrontal cortex. What it has always lacked
    is a hippocampus: a system that encodes your knowledge as compact memory traces (engrams),
    stores them locally and encrypted, and retrieves only what's relevant — deterministically,
    the same answer every time. Graphnosis is that missing piece. (Why the seahorse logo?
    "Hippocampus" is Greek for seahorse — the brain region was named after it in 1564.)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stored as engrams, not files" icon="document">
    Ingested content is encoded into binary engrams in your local Cortex — not stored
    as readable files the AI can rifle through. On recall, Graphnosis surfaces only the
    nodes relevant to what you're asking right now. Your AI gets a precise memory,
    not a document dump.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Private by architecture" icon="approve-check">
    Your Cortex never leaves your device. The only time any content moves is when you are
    actively using an AI client and it asks for context — and even then, only a small,
    semantically filtered excerpt is shared. No Nehloo servers. No background sync. No account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Works with any content" icon="add-document">
    Feed it PDFs, documents, web pages, or quick clips. Graphnosis chunks, embeds,
    and encrypts everything so you can recall it later without remembering
    exact keywords — across sessions, across conversations.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How it works in three steps

1. **Create a Cortex** — choose a local folder, set a passphrase. A 24-word recovery phrase is generated. Nothing is sent anywhere.
2. **Ingest content** — drag in files, paste URLs, or save clips. Graphnosis encodes them as engrams in your encrypted Cortex. The originals stay on your disk.
3. **Your AI remembers** — when you ask your AI a question, Graphnosis retrieves the relevant engrams and delivers them as context. Only the relevant slice. Only in that moment.

## Your data: a clear picture

| Situation | Data leaves your device? |
|---|---|
| Graphnosis is installed but your AI client is closed | No |
| AI client is open but you haven't started a conversation | No |
| You're chatting and recall returns nothing relevant | No |
| Recall fires and returns results | Only the recalled excerpt, only to your AI provider |
| Sensitive-tier engrams | Blocked by default; only a tight cap if you explicitly share one |

Your Cortex is passive. It does not broadcast, sync in the background, or contact any server. Content moves only when you put it to use — and only the piece relevant to what you're asking, delivered to the AI provider you've chosen to use.
